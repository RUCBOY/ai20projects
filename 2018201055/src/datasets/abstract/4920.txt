Evaluating the implementation of public information disclosure as mandated by Law No. 14 of 20008 on the 34 ministries website in Indonesia. Seven parameters were measured, namely: periodic information (P1), the immediate information (P2), the any time information (P3), the service information announcement (P4), the online service of information request (P5), transparency finance and planning (P6 & P7 ). About 50% having the service of P1 to P4. 35.3% having P5. For P6 and P7, 29.4% and 44.1% having ‘Good’ score. In general, the implementation of Law No. 14 of 2008 is still not satisfactory.
